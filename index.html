Faster, cheaper, more resilient Internet

The Technology

The core technology behind Althea is a blockchain-based routing protocol and payment system that debits and credits cryptocurrency based on a router’s bandwidth usage. You can buy a router with our firmware set up and ready to go, or build one yourself—all of our code is open source.

Althea test deployments are launching now in the US and Colombia, with hundreds of other communities ready to get started. Join us: whether you’ve got a little extra time, technical skills, a commitment to open and accessible Internet, or all of the above, we’d love to hear from you.
